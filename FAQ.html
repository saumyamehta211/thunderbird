FAQ

Q. What is the Vaccination Policy at Thunder Bird Fury?
As of September 22nd, 2021, the Government proposed changes to Regulation 645/21 become effective making the following areas and amenities within the hotel available only to those guests who can provide proof of vaccination. Restaurants* - proof of vaccination is required for dine-in guests *Guests who are not able to provide proof of vaccination are welcome to dine on the patio.

Q. Is the rooftop pool open? How do I reserve a spot?
The rooftop pool on the 28th floor is currently open daily from 7am - 11pm. *Adults only from 9pm - 11pm daily.* The pool capacity is currently set to 24 people to adhere to policies and regulations for COVID-19. 2 hour time slots are available for booking. Reservations are required. Guests who have pre-paid reservations directly through the hotel may pre-book their time slots via email. Guests who have not pre-paid may book their time slots at the front desk upon check-in.

Q. How is Thunder Bird Fury responding to COVID-19?
The safety of our guests and employees remains our top priority. We are training and monitoring employees on a daily basis to ensure comprehensive adherence to the latest guidelines on hygiene and containment. Our health and safety protocols are designed to address a broad spectrum of viruses, including COVID-19. Please note, effective September 22, 2021, as per the government proposed changes to Regulation 645/21, guests will be required to show proof of vaccination to enjoy the following amenities and areas of the property: dinning in at the restaurants, gym, and Screening Room.

Q. I lost something. Do you have a lost and found?
Yes there is a lost and found at the reception.

Q. Is there parking at the hotel?
Yes! Self-parking is available for $35 inclusive of tax per night. If you self-park, be sure to leave your bags with our doorman at the main entrance prior to parking and we can bring them to your room. We recommend parking on Level B2, as this elevator brings you directly to the lobby.

Q. What Makes Hotel X Toronto a Standout?
Service | As a proud member of the Thunder Bird Fury, our urban resort is the latest to join an award-winning family of luxury hotels consistently recognized by guests and travel industry experts for excellence in service.
Features | There’s simply no hotel or resort in Toronto that offers an array of services, amenities, and features quite like Thunder Bird Fury. From diverse dining options to a state-of-the-art sports and wellness complex to the most unique event and wedding venues in Toronto, ours is the ultimate urban resort of Ontario.
Location | From our historic location in Toronto’s Exhibition Place, the best of the downtown core is easily accessible from our hotel. We’re steps from the Enercare Centre, CN Tower, Budweiser Stage, and all the best attractions in Ontario Place, Downtown Toronto, and beyond.

Q. Is Smoking Allowed at Hotel X Toronto?
Thunder Bird Fury is dedicated to providing a healthy, balanced lifestyle for our guests. Smoking is prohibited in all guest rooms and public spaces. The hotel reserves the right to charge guests a minimum of $300.00 for costs incurred by smoking.

Q. How do I submit a review for your hotel?
We greatly appreciate your sharing your reviews, especially on TripAdvisor! If you have any special suggestions on what we could do better, please feel free to contact the General Manager.

Q. Are you pet friendly?
Yes, we love our furry guests and we welcome well behaved dogs, up to 2 per room, and services animals. Your favourite little travel companion deserves a luxurious experience too! Take your furry friend for a serene stroll along the waterfront at Trillium Park or pamper them at the nearby Spaw Boutique! Ask our Concierge about pet-sitting services & doggie friendly activities in one of the most welcoming cities for Very Important Pets! Enjoy the following V.I.P Amenities:
*Doggie Bed
*Water & Food Bowls
*Specialty Treats
*Additional $100 Per Stay
*Dogs are restricted to certain guest room floors in consideration of those guests who prefer a pet free zone. There is a deep cleaning fee, and we ask for an application form to be filled out in advance for each pet.

Q. Can I take my wedding photos at Thunder Bird Fury?
We love wedding photo shoots at Thunder Bird Fury! If your wedding or event is being held on property, at Thunder Bird Fury, we are delighted to coordinate your complimentary photo shoot. For any ques expandtions about your photo shoot for an on-site wedding, please speak to your wedding specialist. If your wedding or event is not taking place on property, unfortunately, we do not allow for off-site wedding or event photo shoots.

Q. I have a room booked at the hotel, can I take pictures during my stay?
Guests are more than welcome to take photos with their phone to document their wonderful vacation. However, photos to be taken with a photographer are not permitted.

Q. Do you have a pool? Do you have to be a guest to use the pool?
We have a 55 foot heated pool on the 28th floor of Hotel X Toronto. We try to keep the pool at a comfortable 80°F (26°C) to 85°F (29°C) depending on the season. As a future amenity, we plan to open a second pool at 10XTO, this time a salt water Junior Olympic size pool (25 meters) which will be available to hotel guests, as well as local members. Our Rooftop pool is located on the 28th floor and is for registered hotel guests only. The pool is available for guests to enjoy from 7 AM - 11 PM (adults only from 9 PM - 11 PM) and is open year-round. Please note, the pool capacity has now been set to 24 people to adhere to policies and regulations. Upon reaching capacity, guests may be placed on a wait list and will be notified once they can be accommodated on the pool deck. Wait times are entirely subject to availability and access to the pool deck cannot be guaranteed.
